Whisky vs. Gin

Some people think whisky is better than gin. Some insist gin is superior to whisky. Let's explore this difference!

Whisky

  • Grain-based spirit
  • Barrel-aged
  • Notes of caramel, vanilla and oak

Gin

  • Grain-based spirit
  • Unaged
  • Notes of juniper, black pepper, and lemon

Notice how both whisky and gin are made from grain-based spirits! Perhaps those quarreling over which is superior should take this in stock and learn to enjoy both!
